# H+ Content Ops Sheet, Monthly Loop v1

## Mission

Ship consistent, operator-first content that builds trust, culture, and demand without burning the team out.

---

## Two-track engine

### Track A, Social Momentum
Fast, frequent, trend-compatible, 10–20s clips.
**Primary tool:** Pollo AI.

### Track B, Brand Authority
Cinematic anchor content, 30–45s.
**Primary tool:** Sora 2.
**Frequency:** 1 per month or every 6 weeks.

---

## Weekly cadence

**5 posts per week**
Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri, Sat.
Sunday optional.

---

## Content pillars

1. **Operator value**
2. **Time and revenue clarity**
3. **Flavor culture**
4. **QR convenience**
5. **Lounge vibe**
6. **Pilot momentum**

---

## The Trust Triad filter

Every post should imply at least **two**:
- **Staff ease**
- **Owner confidence**
- **Customer vibe**

If it only hits one, revise.

---

### Week 1, Identity and clarity

**Goal:** Define H+ in simple, repeatable language.

**Length:** 20–30s  
**Format:** One concept per clip.

**Structure:**
- Hook, 2–3s
- Problem, 3–5s
- H+ action, 8–12s
- Outcome, 3–5s
- Close, 2–3s

2. Save a flavor mix instantly

## Brand voice card

### One-line identity
**Hookah+ powers calm, precise, modern lounge sessions.**

### Tone
Confident, smooth, supportive.  
Playful in small doses.

### Use words like
Flow, calm, precision, session clarity, quiet control, operator-first.

### Avoid words like
Disrupt, revolutionary, AI magic, game-changer.

### Tagline options

- The calm behind the curtain.
- The new rhythm of lounge operations.
- Precision sessions, premium experience.

### Caption formula
Problem + calm solution + culture hint.

**Examples:**
- "Peak hours get noisy. H+ keeps sessions clean."
- "Time drift is revenue drift. We fix that quietly."
- "Your regulars deserve recognition. H+ remembers the mix."
