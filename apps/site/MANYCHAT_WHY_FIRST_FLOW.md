## 🎨 ManyChat Best Practices

1. **Keep messages under 320 characters** when possible (split longer messages)
2. **Use Quick Replies** for navigation (max 3-4 options)
3. **Include links** to full pages for detailed information
4. **Use emojis sparingly** (✅ ❌ ❓ work well for lists)
5. **Test on mobile** - most users will be on mobile devices
